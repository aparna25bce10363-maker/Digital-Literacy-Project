Stay Safe Online Checklist:

1. Never share your OTP or UPI PIN with anyone.
2. Do not click on unknown or suspicious links.
3. Always verify calls claiming to be from banks.
4. Use strong and unique passwords.
5. Enable two-factor authentication where possible.
6. Regularly check your bank transactions.
7. Avoid using public Wi-Fi for financial transactions.
8. Install apps only from trusted sources.

Financial Safety Tips:

9. Double-check UPI IDs before sending money.
10. Do not approve unknown payment requests.

Report Cybercrime:

- National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in
- Helpline Number: 1930
